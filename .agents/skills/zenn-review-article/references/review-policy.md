# Review policy

- Blocker: fabricated or contradicted claim, secret exposure, unsafe instructions, missing core evidence, broken article format, or publication would be misleading.
- Warning: reproducibility gap, material ambiguity, uncited external claim, missing limitation, or deterministic check failure that can be fixed from existing evidence.
- Suggestion: optional clarity or style improvement.

`pass` requires blocker 0 and warning 0. Every non-suggestion finding must say what to change and which evidence permits the change.
